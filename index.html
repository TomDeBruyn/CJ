Concrete Jungle

Antwerp a city with many aspects. This City means a lot to many people but what does it mean to the people on wheels, our skaters? As for locals in "'T stad" but also to newcomers in Antwerp, our guide Concrete Jungle, could be a great tool.
The city well known for Park Spoor Noord because of its famous skate bowl, as well as its lesser known urban skate spots. With our guide and our app you and us together can discover these spots.
You’ll be directly accompanied to the closest skate spots or a nice piece of concrete to cruise your longboard. Discover Antwerp from scratch to the fullest. All skate shops, skate spots and the best local diners. Use the coupons at the back of our guide and use our stickers when you visit a skate spot. Post you photo’s and your awesome experiences on our own platform!
